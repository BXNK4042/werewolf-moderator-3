## Amulet of Protection

**Team:** Special

**Description:**
The player holding the Amulet of Protection cannot be killed. During each day, the holder must secretly pass the Amulet to another living player. If it is not passed, the Amulet is destroyed and removed from the game.

**Win Condition:**
None. The Amulet is an item, not a team.

---

## Apprentice Seer

**Team:** Village

**Description:**
The Apprentice Seer has no special power while the Seer is alive. If the Seer dies, the Apprentice Seer immediately becomes the Seer and uses the Seer's ability for the rest of the game.

**Win Condition:**
Eliminate all Werewolves.

---

## Aura Seer

**Team:** Village

**Description:**
Each night, the Aura Seer examines one player and learns whether they possess a special role rather than being a normal Villager or Werewolf. In the alternate version, the moderator instead reveals whether the chosen player belongs to the Village team.

**Win Condition:**
Eliminate all Werewolves.

---

## Beholder

**Team:** Village

**Description:**

**Win Condition:**

## Big Bad Wolf

**Team:** Werewolf

**Description:**
If the Werewolves' chosen victim is sitting next to the Big Bad Wolf, the Big Bad Wolf may also kill any combination of their adjacent neighbors that night, as directed by the moderator. If the original attack is redirected, this additional ability does not occur.

**Win Condition:**
Eliminate the Village until the Werewolf team controls the game.

## Black Wolf

**Win Condition:**
Eliminate the Village until the Werewolf team controls the game.
